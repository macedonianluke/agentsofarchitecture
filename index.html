About

Agents of Architecture is a design studio focussed on craft, and imbueing space with rawness and a sense of place and whimsy.

We are a small team of concept designers & architects with a HQ in Mount Macedon, Victoria, Australia.

Farmer's Daughters

80 Collins Street, Melbourne, Australia, 2023

A multi-level experiential restaurant and bar design that celebrates the theatrical aspects of culinary artistry. This space exposes the function and artistry of food preparation, creating an immersive dining experience. From open kitchens to strategically placed prep stations, every touchpoint is designed to showcase the movement and craft behind each dish, turning dining into a captivating performance.

Farmer's Daughters Victoria

Federation Square, Melbourne CBD, Australia, 2023

A culinary journey through Victoria, nestled in the heart of Melbourne's iconic Federation Square. This tourist hub offers a unique gastronomic experience, allowing visitors to explore the state's diverse flavors without leaving the city. The design revolves around a central, open kitchen with campfire cooking, creating a theatrical dining atmosphere. With stunning views of the Yarra River, guests can indulge in a curated menu that showcases the best of Victoria's food and beverages, all while witnessing the artistry of chefs at work in the carefully designed, exposed kitchen space.

Zonzo Estate: The Chapel

Yarra Valley, Victoria, Australia, 2022

Nestled in the picturesque Yarra Valley, The Chapel at Zonzo Estate is a striking sculptural form rising out of the green hillside. Designed to create an intimate and immersive experience for weddings and special events, the chapel features floor-to-ceiling windows that frame breathtaking views of the vineyard and rolling hills. The minimalist interior, adorned with locally sourced materials, creates a serene and elegant atmosphere. The Chapel's unique design allows it to be a blank canvas for personalization while maintaining its own architectural integrity, making it the perfect backdrop for unforgettable moments.

Zonzo Estate: Negozio Del Officio

Yarra Valley, Victoria, Australia, 2023

Negozio Del Officio, or "The Office Shop," is a unique addition to the Zonzo Estate in the Yarra Valley. This multifunctional space serves as both a tasting room and a retail area, offering visitors a chance to sample and purchase Zonzo's finest wines. The design seamlessly blends rustic charm with modern elegance, featuring reclaimed timber, polished concrete floors, and floor-to-ceiling windows that showcase the stunning vineyard views. Custom-built tasting bars and retail displays create an inviting atmosphere for wine enthusiasts to explore and enjoy the estate's offerings. The space also doubles as an intimate venue for private events, making it a versatile and essential part of the Zonzo Estate experience.

Caffeine Trader

Westfield Shopping Centre, Melbourne, Australia, 2023

Caffeine Trade redefines the shopping centre café experience with its minimal, lightweight, and impactful design. Situated in a bustling Westfield, this coffee bar and meeting point serves as an oasis of calm amidst retail excitement. The design emphasizes clean lines and a neutral palette, creating a visually striking yet unobtrusive space. Innovative use of materials like brushed steel, light wood, and glass contributes to the airy feel, while strategic lighting enhances the ambiance. The layout optimizes flow, accommodating both quick coffee runs and leisurely meetings. Caffeine Trade stands as a testament to how thoughtful, minimalist design can create a powerful impact in a high-traffic commercial setting.
