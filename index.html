Agents of Architecture

About

Agents of Architecture is a design studio focussed on craft, and imbueing space with rawness and a sense of place and whimsy.

We are a small team of concept designers & architects with a HQ in Mount Macedon, Victoria, Australia.

Stella The Stargazer

Victoria, Australia, 2022

A mobile event space, to evoke an emotional response and reconnect with the natural world.

Retreat

Port Fairy, Australia, 2024

A boutique Australian prefabricated home, distilled from the quintessential vernacular of the Australian rural landscape. Handcrafted details and raw materials that strip back to the essence of home and remove the unnecessary noise of modernity.

Brumby

High Country, Australia, 2023

A rugged, off-grid cabin inspired by the wild horses of the Australian Alps. Blending seamlessly with its surroundings, Brumby offers a unique retreat that embodies the spirit of freedom and resilience.

Little Fella

Victoria, Australia, 2023

A compact, eco-friendly tiny home that embodies the spirit of Australian ingenuity. Designed for minimal environmental impact and maximum comfort, Little Fella proves that great things come in small packages.

Weekender

Coastal Victoria, Australia, 2024

A modern interpretation of the classic Australian beach house. Designed for relaxation and connection with nature, Weekender offers a perfect escape from city life, blending indoor and outdoor living spaces seamlessly.

11 Churchill Street

Williamstown North, Australia, 2024

A bold and forthright office addition that celebrates exposed structural steel. This project seamlessly blends industrial aesthetics with functional design, creating a striking workspace that stands out in Williamstown North's architectural landscape.

Farmer's Daughters

80 Collins Street, Melbourne, Australia, 2023

A multi-level experiential restaurant and bar design that celebrates the theatrical aspects of culinary artistry...

Farmer's Daughters Victoria

Federation Square, Melbourne CBD, Australia, 2023

A culinary journey through Victoria, nestled in the heart of Melbourne's iconic Federation Square. This tourist hub offers a unique gastronomic experience, allowing visitors to explore the state's diverse flavors without leaving the city.

Zonzo Estate: The Chapel

Yarra Valley, Victoria, Australia, 2022

Nestled in the picturesque Yarra Valley, The Chapel at Zonzo Estate is a striking sculptural form rising out of the green hillside. Designed to create an intimate and immersive experience for weddings and special events.

Zonzo Estate: Negozio Del Officio

Yarra Valley, Victoria, Australia, 2023

Negozio Del Officio, or "The Office Shop," is a unique addition to the Zonzo Estate in the Yarra Valley. This multifunctional space serves as both a tasting room and a retail area, offering visitors a chance to sample and purchase Zonzo's finest wines.

Zonzo Estate: Negozio Del Vino

Zonzo Estate: Spritz Bar"

Yarra Valley, Victoria, Australia, Coming Soon

Get ready for a refreshing addition to Zonzo Estate! The upcoming Spritz Bar promises to be a vibrant and stylish destination for lovers of this classic Italian aperitif.

Caffeine Trade

Westfield Shopping Centre, Melbourne, Australia, 2023

Caffeine Trade redefines the shopping centre café experience with its minimal, lightweight, and impactful design. Situated in a bustling Westfield, this coffee bar and meeting point serves as an oasis of calm amidst retail excitement.
